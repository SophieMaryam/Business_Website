Opening hours

S&S Spa opens from noon to 8.00 pm from Tuesday to Friday and from 10.00 am to 8.00 pm on weekends. The spa is closed on Monday. If you have any questions please contact the team of S&S Spa via s&sspa@gmail.nl or +31 20 938 47 77.

Before Coming

Important information to bear in mind when visiting S&S SPA

To preserve the tranquil environment of the spa, we respectfully ask you to switch off your mobile phones, as well as other electronic devices. We also request that you refrain from speaking too loudly in the spa.

Nagomi Spa facilities are accessible for guests from the age of 18.

We will provide you with a bathrobe, slippers and towels.

It is possible to cancel your treatment up to 24 hours prior to your treatment. In the event of a late cancellation or a no-show, cancellation costs are applicable.
